About HK-FUSION

HK-FUSION is a next-generation energy research corporation dedicated to the development of Artificial Sun technology through controlled nuclear fusion. Our objective is to achieve stable, safe, and commercially viable fusion power that can supply clean energy for centuries.

Core Technologies

Magnetic Confinement (Tokamak)

Advanced plasma containment using superconducting magnetic fields.

Laser Inertial Confinement

High-energy laser compression systems designed to achieve ignition.

Plasma Stability Engineering

Real-time AI-assisted plasma control for sustained reactions.

Fusion Materials Science

Radiation-resistant alloys and heat-resistant reactor walls.

Grid Integration

Fusion-to-grid conversion systems for national infrastructure deployment.

Research & Development

  • High-temperature superconductors
  • AI plasma diagnostics
  • Quantum simulation for reactor optimization
  • Neutron shielding materials
  • Compact modular fusion reactors

Commercial Roadmap

  • Phase 1 – Experimental plasma ignition
  • Phase 2 – Net positive energy output
  • Phase 3 – Pilot fusion reactor facility
  • Phase 4 – Commercial power plant deployment

Global Impact

Fusion eliminates carbon emissions, reduces fossil fuel dependency, and provides limitless power for AI, robotics, data centers, and future space industries.

Partnership & Investment

HK-FUSION collaborates with governments, research institutions, energy providers, and strategic investors.
